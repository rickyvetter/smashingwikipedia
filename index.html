About

Smashing Wikipedia is all about a convenient way to browse and skim random Wiki articles. Use the arrow keys, A and D, or click a random button to load the next article. Use 1-6 to load one of the pre-selected articles, or click on the title you find interesting. Press Enter to go to the full Wikipedia page for the topic you are currently looking at.
